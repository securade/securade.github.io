Maximizing Safety with AI: Explore the Cutting-Edge Features of Securade.ai Edge App

Posted on October 20, 2023 by Arjun Krishnamurthy

In the ever-evolving world of technology, ensuring safety in various environments has become increasingly critical. This is where Securade.ai Edge App comes into play, a groundbreaking solution that combines advanced AI with real-time monitoring to enhance safety and compliance in numerous settings. Here's an in-depth look at what this innovative app offers.

Introduction to Securade.ai Edge App

Securade.ai Edge App is designed to transform how businesses and organizations monitor and enforce safety protocols. With its user-friendly admin interface, it allows you to seamlessly configure safety policies and manage camera feeds, ensuring a comprehensive safety system.

Key Features:

  1. Admin Interface: The first point of interaction with the app, where you can set up and manage various feeds and define your safety policies.
  2. Camera Feed Integration: Preview connected camera feeds in real-time, providing an unfiltered view of ongoing activities.
  3. Policy Configuration: Customize safety policies to fit specific needs, including:
    • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Detection
    • Proximity Detection
    • Zone Management

Zone Management:

A standout feature, Zone Management, allows you to define areas where human entry is restricted. Once a zone is set, the app can detect and alert if people enter these designated areas.

Applying and Monitoring Policies

After defining your policies, you can apply them to the live feed. Load the policy file, and the app will overlay the policy on the live camera feed. This feature ensures that your policies are working as intended in real-time.

Settings and Customizations:

  1. Model Selection: Choose the AI model that best suits your needs.
  2. Threshold Settings: Adjust confidence thresholds for detections.
  3. Privacy Options: Options to mask faces for privacy concerns.
  4. Alerts Configuration: Set up a Telegram group or other channels to receive instant alerts.

Once satisfied with the settings, you can start the server. The app will continuously monitor for safety violations and send alerts as configured.

Reporting and Analytics

An integral part of Securade.ai Edge App is its robust reporting system. You can access a dashboard that provides detailed insights into safety violations over time. This feature is invaluable for understanding trends and areas that require more attention.

  • Date-wise Violation Tracking: See which dates had the most safety violations.
  • Trend Analysis: Analyze patterns in safety breaches to implement better strategies.

Securade.ai Edge App is more than just a monitoring tool; it's a comprehensive solution for enhancing safety and compliance in various environments. With its advanced AI technology, intuitive interface, and detailed analytics, it is set to revolutionize the way we approach safety management.

FAQ

Common questions.

If you don't see your question here, drop us a note.

What is Securade.ai?
We run generative AI on top of your CCTV feeds and watch for the stuff that turns into accidents: hard hats missing, people standing where forklifts move, that kind of thing. The point is to catch it before someone gets hurt, instead of pulling footage out after the fact. We work with construction, manufacturing, oil and gas, and logistics teams.
Does Securade.ai work with existing CCTV cameras?
Yes. HUB talks to any RTSP/ONVIF camera, so you can keep the cameras you have and just point them at it. No rip-and-replace.
Is Securade.ai HUB open source?
Yes, HUB is on GitHub at github.com/securade/hub. We also sell a commercial license for production deployments with priority support, in case that fits better — you can buy one here.
How does generative AI video analytics work?
The short version: instead of labelling thousands of bounding boxes by hand, you describe what you're after in plain language or show the model five example pictures. The model figures out the rest. That's how we fine-tune detectors for site-specific PPE, exclusion zones, and unsafe acts in minutes, not weeks.
What industries does Securade.ai support?
Mostly construction sites, factories, oil & gas plants, and logistics yards. But honestly, if there are cameras and people moving around machinery, the same approach works. One of our weirder deployments was at a preschool in Singapore (to watch the playground), which we did not see coming.
